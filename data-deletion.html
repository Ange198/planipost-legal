Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: July 14, 2026

PlaniPost, developed by AmeDuDev, respects your privacy and allows you to request the deletion of your personal data at any time.

How to request data deletion

If you connected your Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Google, YouTube or TikTok account to PlaniPost and wish to delete your data, simply send a request to:

contact@amedudev.com

Please include:

  • Your PlaniPost email address.
  • Your connected social network account.
  • A request stating that you want your data deleted.

What data will be deleted?

  • Your social account connections.
  • OAuth access tokens.
  • Stored refresh tokens.
  • Scheduled publications associated with your account.
  • Your PlaniPost account (upon request).

Processing time

Deletion requests are generally processed within a reasonable period after verification of the account owner's identity.

Questions

If you have any questions regarding your privacy or your data, contact us at:

contact@amedudev.com
